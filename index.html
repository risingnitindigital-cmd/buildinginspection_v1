Frequently Asked Questions


What does a building inspection include?
A full assessment of structural integrity, moisture, pests, roofing, plumbing observations, electrical observations, safety hazards, and renovation quality.
Do you offer same-day reports?
Yes! All reports are delivered within 24 hours, and we often get them to you on the same day as the inspection.
Can I attend the inspection?
Absolutely. We encourage clients to attend, especially towards the end, so the inspector can walk you through any major findings.
Do you check for termites?
Yes. Our Building & Pest inspections include a full timber pest assessment using visual checks, moisture meters, and thermal imaging.
